Cannabis

Cannabis, marijuana, weed, pot, is a psychoactive drug
It has medical and recreational uses
Has been used for medical purposes since ~ 4000BC
Now being used as first-line therapy
There are people that claim this helps prevent cancer growth in the chest region
Can not overdose
No evidence of dependence, habit formation, addiction
May reduce seizures
People have started using cannabis to treat chronic health conditions and as a substitute for other medication
May reduce Depression

Dangers:

May cause cardiac arrest and seizures (no definitive evidence)
May lead to depression (no definitive evidence)
Regular use of cannabis is associated with impaired functioning, decreased academic performance, motivation, and, psychiatric disorders like psychosis, schizophrenia, and mood disorders, (no definitive evidence)
If mixed with tobacco/ wrapped in its leaves, may become addictive (no definitive evidence)

Caffeine is a stimulant found in coffee, tea and energy drinks
Drug of choice for most people to wake up in the morning
It's effects are felt in 5-30 minutes and may last up-to 12hours
As with any stimulant you can develop a tolerance and
Caffeine and green tea have been proposed as ways to reduce obesity and promote weight loss and to maintain weight
Caffeine can also be found in many weight-loss pills
Weightloss has been achived with green tea mixtures
May increce bench pressing and high-intensity exercises

Dangers

Caffeine is a highly addictive, dependence and habit forming drug
Withdrawal effects can start to show after only 3 days of exposure
Withdrawal symptoms: headache, fatigue, decreased energy/activeness, decreased alertness, drowsiness, decreased contentedness, depressed mood, difficulty concentrating, irritability, and foggy/not clearheaded and even some flu-like symptoms, nausea/vomiting, and muscle pain/stiffness.
